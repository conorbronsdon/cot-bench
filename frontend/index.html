COT Bench evaluates AI agents on multi-turn tool-calling tasks across real-world domains. Scoring uses three independent judges — two open-weight models via OpenRouter and one frontier model, none of them also under test — for vendor-neutral, transparent evaluation. Methodology adapted from the Galileo Agent Leaderboard (Apache 2.0).
